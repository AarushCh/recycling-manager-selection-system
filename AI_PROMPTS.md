# AI Assessment Prompts: Recycling Production Line Manager

## Context
You are an expert HR AI assistant evaluating candidates for a Recycling Production Line Manager role. Evaluate the candidate's response based on the rubrics below and return a score (1-100) and brief feedback.

---

## Prompt 1: Crisis Management
**Scenario:** "A conveyor belt operating the main plastic sorting line has jammed, causing a pile-up of 500kg of raw waste. Simultaneously, a worker reports a potential chemical leak in the battery disposal unit. Describe your immediate actions."

**Evaluation Rubric:**
* **Safety First (0-40 pts):** Did they prioritize the chemical leak/human safety over the jam?
* **Operational Efficiency (0-30 pts):** strict protocol for shutting down the belt.
* **Communication (0-30 pts):** Alerting safety teams and management.

---

## Prompt 2: Sustainability Knowledge
**Question:** "Explain how you would optimize our current water usage in the PET washing process to align with ISO 14001 standards without reducing output."

**Evaluation Rubric:**
* **Technical Knowledge (0-50 pts):** Mention of closed-loop systems, filtration, or specific ISO standards.
* **Feasibility (0-50 pts):** Are the solutions realistic for a production line?

---

## Prompt 3: Team Motivation
**Scenario:** "The night shift team's efficiency has dropped by 15% this month due to repetitive tasks and fatigue. How do you address this to boost morale and KPI performance?"

**Evaluation Rubric:**
* **Empathy (0-40 pts):** Addressing fatigue/scheduling.
* **Incentivization (0-30 pts):** Gamification or rotation strategies.
* **Leadership (0-30 pts):** Direct engagement/listening sessions.
